BASTA is a group of alumni committed to ending sexual assault at Brown. We have created a formalized network to communicate with students and maintain pressure on the administration. Our goal is to avoid the historical amnesia which forces student activsts at Brown to reinvent the wheel every four years when it comes to demanding change from the administration. We are committed to working in solidarity with students to demand improved resources for survivors and harsher sanctions against perpetrators.

Brown can no longer get away with failing its students. Alumni are watching.
